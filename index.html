The Eiffel Tower

Constructed in 1889 as the enterance of the World's Fair the Eiffel Tower is a world icon for France sitting in the center of Paris. Engineer Gustave Eiffel designed it as a wrought iron lattice tower and it was initially criticized for its design. It has turned out to be the most visited paid monument in the world with about 7 million visitors. Standing at 1,063 ft tall it is by far the tallest structure in Paris.
